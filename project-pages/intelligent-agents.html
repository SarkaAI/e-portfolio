Intelligent Agents
Exploring agent-based computing : the theory, tools, and techniques behind autonomous systems that perceive their environment and act to achieve their goals.
Learning Outcomes
This module develops a critical understanding of intelligent agents : software systems that perceive their environment and act autonomously to achieve their goals. Key learning outcomes include identifying and analysing different agent architectures, applying agent techniques to real-world problems involving uncertainty and risk, and deploying appropriate tools for the design and implementation of agent-based systems.
Emphasis is placed on legal, social, ethical, and professional considerations when designing autonomous systems, alongside the practical skills required to contribute effectively as a member of a development team in a virtual professional environment.
