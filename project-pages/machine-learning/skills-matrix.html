Professional Skills Matrix and Action Plan
My self-assessment and my plan for ongoing development following the Machine Learning module.
Overview
This module has supported the development of a range of technical and professional skills relevant to machine learning and its real-world application. I strengthened my analytical and critical thinking skills through working with datasets, interpreting model outputs, and recognising limitations in different approaches. My technical skills improved in areas such as data preprocessing, exploratory analysis, and understanding core techniques including regression, clustering, and neural networks.
I also developed my ability to communicate complex ideas clearly through ePortfolio activities and discussion forums, where I was required to explain concepts and engage with different perspectives. The group project contributed to my teamwork and collaboration skills, particularly in working within a structured role and contributing to a shared outcome in a virtual environment. In addition, engaging with ethical discussions increased my awareness of the broader impact and responsible use of machine learning systems.
Moving forward, I aim to deepen my understanding of more advanced modelling techniques and strengthen my confidence in implementing them independently. I would also like to further develop my statistical understanding to better interpret model behaviour. As part of my action plan, I will continue working on independent projects and look for opportunities to apply these skills in practical contexts, ensuring a stronger connection between technical knowledge and real-world application.
