Numerical Analysis
Mastering numerical methods and computational techniques for solving mathematical problems and scientific applications.
Learning Outcomes
This module developed essential numerical methods used in scientific computing and machine learning, including root-finding techniques, numerical integration and differentiation, linear systems, interpolation, and the analysis of numerical stability and error propagation.
Learners gained hands-on experience implementing these methods in code, evaluating their computational efficiency and accuracy, and understanding how numerical techniques underpin modern AI algorithms, optimisation routines, and data analysis pipelines.
