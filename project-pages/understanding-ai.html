Understanding Artificial Intelligence
Exploring the fundamentals of AI, including key concepts, algorithms, and applications shaping the future of technology.
Learning Outcomes
This module introduced the core concepts of artificial intelligence, including its history, foundational algorithms, and the ethical considerations that shape its development. Key outcomes included understanding search and optimisation techniques, knowledge representation, reasoning under uncertainty, and the role of machine learning within the broader AI landscape.
Emphasis was placed on critically evaluating AI applications across industries, recognising the strengths and limitations of different approaches, and considering the societal and ethical implications of deploying AI systems responsibly.
