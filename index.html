My Background / About Me
I am currently pursuing an MSc in Artificial Intelligence with the University of Essex Online, building on a strong foundation as a senior security researcher in offensive cybersecurity. My academic journey explores key areas such as ethical AI, inclusive design, and explainable AI. I’m particularly interested in applying AI within cybersecurity and healthcare contexts, and I bring practical insight from years of hands-on experience with critical infrastructure or the dark web. This portfolio showcases my progress, reflections, and key artefacts developed throughout the programme.
